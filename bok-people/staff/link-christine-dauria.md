# Christine D'Auria

Assistant Director of the Learning Lab

![Headshot of Christine](/sites/g/files/omnuum6756/files/styles/hwp_4_5__480x600/public/shadowbok/files/default_1.4.2.jpg?itok=l3vsVmXh)

As Assistant Director of the Learning Lab, Christine collaborates with students, faculty, and the Learning Lab team to develop multimodal projects that have applications in a range of courses and departments across the College and Harvard Griffin Graduate School of Arts and Sciences. In this capacity, Christine works closely with the Bok Graduate Fellows (BGFs), supporting and documenting BGF projects. Christine received her MA in Literary and Cultural Studies from Carnegie Mellon University and holds a PhD in American Studies from Boston University. When not at the Learning Lab, Christine can be found watching movies.
