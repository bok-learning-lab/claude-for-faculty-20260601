# Dani Duke

Learning Lab Project Manager

![Dani Duke Headshot](/sites/g/files/omnuum6756/files/styles/hwp_4_5__480x600/public/2025-02/Dani_Duke_Headshot.png?itok=c1tETc0L)

Dani manages the Learning Lab’s many projects, overseeing partnerships, resources, and workflows to support creative approaches to teaching and learning. She also coordinates tasks across the team to ensure smooth operations and effective collaboration. Dani earned a Bachelor of Science in Human Services from Northeastern University in 2018 and has been at the Bok Center ever since. For any questions related to the Learning Lab, please reach out to [learninglab@fas.harvard.edu](mailto:learninglab@fas.harvard.edu).
