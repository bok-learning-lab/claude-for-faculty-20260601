# Jordan Koffman

Assistant Director of the Learning Lab

![Jordan Koffman](/sites/g/files/omnuum6756/files/styles/hwp_4_5__480x600/public/shadowbok/files/46385327031_ba6bd2c5b4_o_1.jpg?itok=VnLQ3WQC)

As Assistant Director of the Learning Lab, Jordan facilitates internal working groups of fellows and staff to develop in house capabilities and actualize the learning and design goals of our faculty collaborators. She received her Masters in Education from the Harvard Graduate School of Education’s Arts in Education Program, and is invested in curating learning environments that leverage multi-modal practices for communicating academic ideas. She brings her experience as an artist, educator, and convener and her skills utilizing the technical mediums of the Learning Lab to each faculty consultation, course workshop, and bespoke design challenge that emerges. Above all, Jordan works to create a warm and encouraging environment for students and faculty alike.
